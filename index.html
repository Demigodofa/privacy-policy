Privacy Policy for Material Guardian

Material Guardian is an offline-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

Data Storage

All data created within the app, including jobs, reports, photos, scans, and signatures, is stored locally on the user’s device.

Permissions

The app requests access to the device camera solely for capturing photos and scanning materials as part of the inspection workflow.

Data Sharing

The app does not automatically share any data. Users may choose to export files to their device storage or share them using available system share options.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.
