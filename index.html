Material Guardian

Privacy Policy

Material Guardian is an offline-first receiving inspection app. In the current release, jobs, receiving reports, photos, scans, signatures, and exported packet files stay on your device unless you intentionally export or share them.

The app now also supports backend-managed account access, organization membership, seats, and store-backed subscription state. This policy reflects that backend-backed release behavior.

What stays on your device

Job content, draft reports, photos, scans, signatures, and exported packets are stored locally on your device in the current release. Cross-device cloud file sync and cloud file recovery are planned for a later opt-in upgrade and are not part of the current release behavior.

Account and backend data

If you sign in, Material Guardian may store backend account and access data needed to operate the service, including:

  • your email address and sign-in session state
  • email-code authentication challenges and session tokens
  • organization membership, seat assignment, and workspace role state
  • subscription and entitlement state linked to Google Play or the App Store when store-backed access is active

Camera, scanning, and exports

The app may request camera or document-scanning access only when you choose to capture material photos or scan MTR/CoC documents. Exported files are written to app storage and may also be written to a device-accessible folder such as Downloads/MaterialGuardian when you export packets.

Sharing

Material Guardian does not automatically publish your reports or media. Files are shared only when you explicitly choose an export or system share destination.

Third-party services

Material Guardian does not use third-party analytics or ad tracking in the current release. The service may rely on third-party infrastructure for account and billing operations, such as:

  • Google Play or the App Store for store-backed subscriptions
  • a configured email delivery provider for sign-in codes, support messages, or workspace invite emails
  • backend hosting and database infrastructure used to run account, entitlement, and organization features

Account deletion and retention

If account deletion is available in your release channel, use the in-app delete-account flow to remove the backend login tied to your email. Store subscriptions may still need to be canceled separately in Google Play or the App Store.

Business workspace records can require additional retention or ownership handling before deletion is allowed. For public deletion guidance, use the account deletion page.
