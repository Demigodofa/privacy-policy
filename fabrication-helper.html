Fabrication Helper

Privacy Policy

Fabrication Helper is a local-first fabrication calculator and reference app. The current release is designed to work without an account, without backend sign-in, and without cloud sync.

The app is planned as a one-time paid download. Store purchase and payment handling are managed by Google Play or the App Store, not by Fabrication Helper inside the app.

Related page: Terms and Safety Notes.

Data stored on your device

The current release may store app preferences locally on your device, including which tools you choose to show on the home screen.

Calculator inputs, layout values, and reference lookups are processed locally in the app. Fabrication Helper does not send those values to a Welders Helper backend in the current release.

No account in the current release

The current Fabrication Helper release does not create user accounts, collect email addresses, manage organization seats, or require sign-in.

Because there is no Fabrication Helper account in the current release, there is no account-deletion flow for this app. If a future release adds accounts or company cloud records, this policy and the public deletion instructions will be updated before that release ships.

Purchases

If you buy Fabrication Helper through Google Play or the App Store, the store handles payment, receipts, refunds, purchase history, taxes, and account-level purchase records under that store's policies.

Fabrication Helper does not receive your payment card details.

Permissions and sharing

The current release does not need camera, contacts, microphone, location, broad file storage, or advertising tracking permissions for its local calculator workflow.

If future releases add export, sharing, camera, document scan, notifications, or cloud record features, those permissions and data flows will be disclosed here and in the app store declarations before release.

Analytics and advertising

Fabrication Helper does not use third-party advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs in the current release.

System backup

Your device operating system may back up app data or preferences depending on your device settings, Google account, Apple account, or backup configuration. Those backups are controlled by the operating system and store account settings.
