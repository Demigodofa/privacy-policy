Fabrication Helper Terms and Safety Notes

Fabrication Helper provides fabrication calculators, layout aids, and reference tools for field and shop use.

Related page: Privacy Policy.

Use limits

Calculations, diagrams, and reference values are aids for layout and checking. They are not stamped engineering approval, code interpretation, shop authorization, or a substitute for qualified review.

  • Verify all results against drawings, specifications, purchase requirements, supplier data, governing standards, and shop procedures before ordering, cutting, welding, or releasing work.
  • Reference data such as pipe tables, schedule aliases, material groupings, weld symbols, structural shapes, steam data, and formulas must be checked against the applicable source and edition before production use.
  • Fitting takeoffs, allowances, gaps, trims, and shop practices can differ by fitting type, vendor, specification, and job requirement.

Purchases and refunds

The first release is planned as a one-time paid app through the platform store. Purchases, refunds, tax handling, and purchase history are managed by the store that sold the app.

Future cloud features

Company WPS, WPQ, PQR, mechanical test report, and QC-controlled record features are future cloud modules. They are not part of the current local calculator release unless a later app version clearly enables and discloses them.
